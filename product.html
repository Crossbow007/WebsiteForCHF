Our Products

We meticulously select top-notch ingredients from local suppliers, Europe, and China to craft our premium product line. Featuring hotpot noodles, potato thin noodles, potato jelly, and chili oil, each item boasts a fresh, innovative recipe tailored for Canadian tastes.


Proudly certified as suppliers for a Loblaws sub-supermarket brand, we extend our reach to over 20 Asian supermarkets across Ontario. Our private label, known as "Taste of Home," reflects our dedication to delivering high-quality, flavorful products.
