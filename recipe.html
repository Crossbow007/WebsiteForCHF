Sour and Spicy Potato Starch Noodles

  • Cook potato starch noodles in boiling water, then rinse with cold water and drain.
  • In a bowl, mix 2 tablespoons of chili powder, 1 tablespoon of toasted white sesame seeds, 1 tablespoon of chopped green onions, 2 tablespoons of minced garlic, and a small amount of diced spicy peppers.
  • Heat oil and pour it over the mixture, then add 2 tablespoons of black vinegar, 2 tablespoons of light soy sauce, half a tablespoon of dark soy sauce, and a little less than half a tablespoon of salt and sugar. Mix well.
  • Add half a bowl of hot water to the mixture. Place the cooked potato starch noodles into the bowl, add peanuts, cilantro, and pickled vegetables. Mix well.

    Braised Chicken with Mushrooms

  • Prepare chicken, potato starch noodles, green onions, ginger, garlic, soy sauce (mix of light and dark soy sauce), salt, sugar, and star anise. Cut the chicken into chunks, and cut the green onions, ginger, and garlic into large pieces.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add green onions, ginger, garlic, and star anise. Stir-fry the chicken until most of the moisture is evaporated and it develops a nice brown color.
  • Pour in cooking wine and stir. Add the mixed soy sauce and a small amount of sugar. Continue stirring. Keep the heat high, stir until the chicken is evenly colored. Pour in hot water. Once the broth is boiling, reduce the heat to simmer the chicken.
  • After the chicken is cooked, add mushrooms and potato starch noodles. Cover and simmer for about 10 minutes, then uncover and simmer for another 10 minutes. Adjust the seasoning with salt to taste.

    Potato Jelly

  • Shred cucumber, chop cilantro, peel roasted peanuts, and prepare a sauce with soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, oyster sauce, salt, chili oil, and sesame oil.
  • Cut the cold noodles into strips and mix with the sauce. Add shredded cucumber, cilantro, and peanuts.

    • Hot Pot Noodles

  • Boil water in a pot, add 180g of hot pot base, and let it boil. Taste the broth and add 2 tablespoons of salt if needed. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Add cleaned bean sprouts and cook for 5 minutes. Remove and set aside. Add washed hot pot noodles and cook for 2 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  • Add vinegar to the broth and it's ready to serve.
