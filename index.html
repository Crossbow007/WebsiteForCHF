About CHF
    Kevin immigrated to Kingston from China over three years ago think we missed our traditional foods in China, especially potato noodles that his wife loved. Fresh potato noodles can not be found in Ontario, even in entire Canada, so they were inspired to make it own. As a scientist with a doctorate degree in food science, he is passionate about creating pioneering food products.With his expertise and his wife's love for potato noodles, they have developed many brand new recipes that would meet the needs of the Chinese and local communities in Ontario.
