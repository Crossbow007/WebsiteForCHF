About Us

Hi there! My name is Kevin, and I am the founder of C Health Food Technology Inc. I am here to share the story of how we started a business that produces delicious food products that are healthy and gluten-free. My family and I immigrated to Kingston from China over three years ago, and we think it is a beautiful city to be our new home. However, we missed our traditional foods in China, especially potato noodles, which my wife loved. Fresh potato noodles can not be found in Ontario, even if in entire Canada, so we were inspired to make our own. As a scientist with a doctorate degree in food s cience, I am passionate about creating pioneering food products. With my expertise and my wife's love for potato noodles, we have developed many brand new recipes that would meet the needs of the Chinese and local communities in Ontario.

As a team, we worked hard to develop some brand-new recipes for Potato Noodles, and Fresh Pasta-like items that are all gluten- free, fat-free, and have a nice and chewy texture. Our products were well-received by the local community, and our food products are now available in over 20 supermarkets in Kingston, Ottawa, and Toronto. We have two types of potato noodles: a wide one that is ideal for hotpot and a thin one that is perfect for making noodle dishes. Additionally, we are excited to announce that in July we will be introducing a new Fresh Pasta-like flavor of noodles that can be prepared in just two minutes, with a smooth and chewy texture, that would be a great substitution for Pasta. We have discovered that there are around one million Chinese people and more than 20 million local people in Ontario, and we want to serve them all. Therefore, we established a new production line that will be more automated and productive. Our success is a result of our commitment to delivering high- quality and fresh food products to our customers. We hope that our story inspires others to follow their passions and never give up on their dreams.

Thank you for reading our story, and we look forward to sharing our delicious food products with you!
